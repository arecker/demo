My Design Process

Your Needs Come First

Whether your are building a new home from scratch or simply looking to give new life to an old space, I will work with you to develop a custom plan to make your space as awesome as you are.

Professional Guidance

Furniture selections, windows, floors, paint, lighting, and cabinetry - let me turn your bold ideas into a comprehensive and flexible design plan.

Trusted Experience

With over 10 years of experience in design, you can trust me to help you avoid costly mistakes and design a space that fits your lifestyle and budget.

My name is Renee Redalen

As an interior designer, wife and mother, I know how to incorporate style and tranquility into each space I design.

My company was founded on the belief that all clients deserve dream spaces at an exceptional value. I know how to incorporate both style and livability into each space I design. My strength is my ability to mix modern with traditional, old with new, to create beautiful spaces with ease.

Being able to assist clients in achieving their dream home is the most rewarding part of my job. I have helped countless clients realize their homes potential whether remodeling, new construction, single spaces, or staging their home for sale.
